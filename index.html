What is HolidayMatch?

HolidayMatch is a women-only home exchange app inspired by the beloved rom-com The Holiday.

Users can browse beautiful homes, swipe to find their perfect match and swap homes with another woman for the same period of time.

Instead of booking hotels or expensive Airbnbs, HolidayMatch allows women to experience a destination like a local while staying in a trusted and verified home.

More Than a Home Exchange

Imagine waking up in a cozy London cottage, spending Christmas in a sunny California beach house, or escaping your everyday routine for a completely new environment.

HolidayMatch is not just about travel. It is about stepping into someone else's world, taking a break from your routine and creating the kind of memories that feel straight out of a movie.
