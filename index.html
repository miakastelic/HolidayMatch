What is HolidayMatch?

HolidayMatch is a women-only home exchange app inspired by the beloved rom-com The Holiday.

Browse beautiful homes around the world and swipe to discover your perfect holiday match. Instead of booking hotels or expensive Airbnbs, women can swap homes with each other for the same period of time and experience a destination like a local.

But HolidayMatch is more than just a home exchange platform. Imagine waking up in a cozy London cottage, spending Christmas in a sunny California beach house, or escaping your everyday routine for a completely new environment.

It is not just about travel. It is about stepping into someone else's world, taking a break from your routine, and creating the kind of memories that feel straight out of a movie.

Every home and member is verified, creating a trusted women-only community where women can travel safely, affordably, and authentically. Because sometimes all you need is a change of scenery to start your own holiday story.
